About Us

The pride and joy of Yomogi the Chef, the Tea Shop provides Hunters with delicious, energizing meals before they set out on hunts. Meals in *Monster Hunter Rise* come in the form of Bunny Dango, specialized rice dumpling treats served on skewers. Bunny Dango provide the Hunter with a generous boost to their maximum Health and Stamina, as well as various bonus skills to aid their hunt. Hunters can choose any combination of three dango per meal from dozens of choices, with more options unlocked by progressing in the game.
